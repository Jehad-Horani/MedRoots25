About IEEE SIGHT


IEEE SIGHT JS (Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology, Jordan Section) is a part of the IEEE Jordan Section, which is a dynamic and vibrant community of professionals dedicated to advancing technology and innovation in Jordan. Our team members has achieved significant activities in the past. In February 2024, we organized the largest congress dedicated to space science in the Region 8 (Africa, Europe, Middle East), known as the IEEE MESYP Congress. Prior to this, we established the first IEEE SIGHT Student Branch group in Jordan in 2019, and we received 2 IEEE Region 8 awards for the best SIGHT SB Group. Additionally, our team has participated in many projects
