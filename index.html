About MedRoots25



MedRoots Summit 25 is an International IEEE SIGHT Conference hosted in Jordan for the first time, highlighting the country's growing role in technology, biomedical engineering, and sustainability within the MENA region. The summit, themed "Empowering the Future through Environmental Development, EcoGenomics, AI, Innovation, Medical Fields, and Engineering," fosters global collaboration among experts, researchers, and students to address key challenges. The event features workshops, keynote speeches, a panel discussion, a competition, and exhibitions, showcasing cutting-edge advancements in AI, environmental engineering, and healthcare innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the summit ?

From the 6th of July till the 8th of July 2025.

Where will the summit be held?

The MedRoots 25 summit will be held at the Conference Palace ( King Hussein Bin Talal Convention Centre (KHBTCC)) at the Hilton Dead Sea Hotel.

Is there a certificate of attendance?

Yes, there is a certificate of attendance at the summit that will be sent to all attendees via their personal email that they used in the form to purchase tickets.

Does the ticket include a hotel room?

No, rooms are booked independently, with a special rate for attendees.

Can I cancel the ticket after completing the purchase ?

Unfortunately, tickets are non-refundable as stated in the terms and conditions.

Is there transportation to and from the venue?

Yes, there will be many stations in Amman, Zarqa and Irbid, and they are free for ticket holders.
