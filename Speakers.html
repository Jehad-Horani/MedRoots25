Field Trip



Ajloun


Ajloun enchants visitors with its rolling green hills and the imposing Ajloun Castle,
a 12th-century fortress built to defend against Crusaders. Beyond its historical allure, Ajloun Forest Reserve provides a serene escape with lush woodlands, diverse wildlife, and scenic hiking trails.



Jerash


Jerash, often called the "Pompeii of the East,"
is a remarkably preserved Roman city with grand colonnaded streets, towering temples, and an iconic Oval Plaza that bring ancient civilizations to life.
